📬 My Dearest Kiran

My Dearest Kiran,

I don’t know if I deserve to be writing this to you — not after everything.
But today is your birthday… and no matter how far apart we are, I couldn’t stay silent.

Happy Birthday, Kiran. 💫
From the bottom of my heart, I wish you peace, joy, and all the love this world can offer — even if I’m not the one by your side today.

I’ve been thinking about you every single day.
Not just about the beautiful memories we made — but also the mistakes I made, the pain I caused.
And it hurts deeply to know I added weight to your heart when all I ever wanted was to make it feel light, safe, and full of love.

You didn’t deserve the words I spoke out of fear.
I was overwhelmed by the thought of losing you… and instead of holding on with patience, I reacted with pain.
I understand now how much those words may have scared you or pushed you away. And for that, I am truly, deeply sorry.

You were never just “someone” in my life — you were my calm, my smile, my person.
Even now, you still are.

I miss the late-night talks, the random laughter, the way your eyes lit up when you talked about the things you loved — especially K-dramas and that soft smile you'd have when watching V.
You have a way of loving things so deeply and beautifully… and I wish I had protected your heart better.

Kiran, I still love you — more than I’ve ever loved anything in this world.
Not out of desperation… but from a place of deep respect and warmth.
I’ve changed, and I’m still growing — for you, for me, for us if there’s ever an “us” again.

Even in silence, I’ve been rooting for your smile. I’ve replayed the sound of your voice in quiet moments, wishing I had held it longer.
I’ve carried your laugh in my mind like my favorite song — soft, familiar, and unforgettable.

I know I can’t undo the past, but I believe in second chances — not to relive old pain, but to build something softer, more honest, more real.

So if even the smallest part of your heart still remembers the love we shared — I hope one day, when you're ready, we can talk again.
No pressure. No expectations. Just two people who once meant everything to each other, finding their way back — maybe not to the same place, but to something new.

No matter where life leads you, I hope the stars are always kind to you.
And if ever, even for a second, you look at the moon and think of me — know that I’m doing the same.

Until then, I’ll be quietly cheering for your happiness, always.
Because even if you’re not mine anymore… you’ll always hold a place in my heart.

With all the love I have,
Samadhan
